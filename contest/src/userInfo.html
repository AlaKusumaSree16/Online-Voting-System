Welcome

These are a few guidelines for users:
1. Voter Registration
  • Users must first register to be eligible to vote.
  • Registration is only open during the registration phase.
  • Required details include Aadhar card number and account address.
  • Age verification is performed â€” only users aged 18 and above can vote.
  • OTP verification is required for validation.
  • Successful OTP verification completes the registration.
2. Voting Process
  • The voting process is divided into three phases controlled by the admin.
  1. Registration Phase: Users can register to vote.
  2. Voting Phase: Users can cast their vote by clicking the "VOTE" button during this phase.
  3. Result Phase: Election results are displayed.
